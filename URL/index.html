How It Works

HeartRate Senior uses your phone's camera and flash to detect light changes from your fingertip and generate an estimated heart rate value.

This feature is designed for general wellness and informational use only.

Important Notice

HeartRate Senior is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

The values shown in the app are estimates for general reference only. If you have any health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
