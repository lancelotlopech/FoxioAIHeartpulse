Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

HeartRate Senior is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We aim to collect as little data as possible and keep everything transparent and easy to understand.

This Privacy Policy explains how HeartRate Senior (the "App") handles your information.

1. Introduction

HeartRate Senior is a wellness and informational app. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

The App processes most data locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to minimize data usage. We only collect and process the following types of data, which are primarily stored on your device:

  • Wellness Data: Estimated heart rate values generated using your device's camera for informational and wellness purposes only.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous system logs provided by iOS for crash reporting and performance improvement (if enabled by your system).

We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity information.

3. HealthKit Integration

HeartRate Senior can optionally integrate with Apple HealthKit.

If you choose to enable this feature:

  • Read Access: We may read your existing heart rate-related records to display your personal history.
  • Write Access: We may save newly generated estimated values to the Apple Health app.
  • Purpose: This data is used solely for personal wellness tracking.

We do not share your HealthKit data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. All HealthKit data remains on your device or in your personal iCloud account (if enabled by you).

4. Camera Usage

The App uses your device's camera and flash to detect light changes from your fingertip and generate an estimated heart rate value for informational and wellness purposes only.

  • Images or video frames are processed in real time and are never saved.
  • No photos or videos are stored or transmitted.
  • The camera is never used when the App is running in the background.

5. Data Storage & Security

All personal wellness data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage technologies.

If you have iCloud enabled, your data may sync across your own Apple devices. This process is fully managed by Apple, and we do not have access to your iCloud account or content.

6. App Tracking Transparency

We may request permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. This permission is used to:

  • Improve the App experience
  • Provide relevant content
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

You can allow or deny this request when prompted. Your choice will not affect the core functionality of the App. You can change this setting anytime in:

Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

7. Medical Disclaimer

This App is for wellness and informational purposes only.

It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional if you have any health concerns.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the App. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
